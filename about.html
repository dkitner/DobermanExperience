About

I am a Doberman owner, lover, and advocate of this lovely breed for over 40 years. In those years, I have learned many lessons, healthcare solutions, and behavior tips that I think will benefit other Doberman owners. I am sharing the Dobermanexperience.com as a posting point to share my experiences.
