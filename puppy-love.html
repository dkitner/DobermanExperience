Giardia Warning: Also test for Giardia, as this is even more common today than worms! If pup has a bad case you may need metronidazole and panacur together. You can pick up Safeguard (which is the same as Panacur) at Tractor Supply (TSC) — my experienced trusted vet advised me on this.

Beware of 'Chain' Vets trying to cure Giardia with only a few days of Panacur as this rarely works and you will spend months trying to get rid of it as your dog builds up a resistance, which makes it even more difficult to kill the parasite, or to get it out of your lawn (reinfection)!!! I learned this the hard way and spent 8 months trying to get rid of it!!! I switched back to my old vet and it was gone within 2 months.

I compared prices vet to vet and the 'Chain vets' are out to make money! I personally have been through this and wasted thousands at a Chain vet, because it was close to home. This last year I went back to my trusted vet who is an hour away and worth every mile. I am back to having peace of mind with my dogs and spending less on BETTER care! Live and Learn, I say.

I will drive more miles to go to the better vet! :) I always appreciate years of experience over textbook newbies.
