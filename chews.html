Chews

Best Chews

Choosing the right chew matters as much as choosing the right food. Here's what we recommend — and what to avoid.

DO

Beef Cheek Roll — Tula loves these! Beef cheek is made from the cow's cheek muscle which makes it nutritious and digestible!

Raw Meaty Bones — Frozen femurs can be purchased at your local butcher and are loaded with nutrient rich bone marrow.

DON'T

Rawhide — Made from the inner layer of a hide, rawhide is not digestible and can be a choking hazard!

Special Needs

If your dog has renal issues you may want to opt for a chew without protein. A chew made from vegetables, rice, or sweet potatoes are safe options.

Mattie's Treats is a good biscuit for dogs with renal issues! Look for choices low in protein and phosphorus.
